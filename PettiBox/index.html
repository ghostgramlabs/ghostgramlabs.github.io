Why PettiBox?

Every day you come across things worth keeping: an article you'll read later, a recipe from Instagram, a screenshot of a receipt, a PDF from work, a product you might buy. They end up scattered across browser bookmarks, chat apps, your gallery and your downloads folder — and when you need them, they're gone.

PettiBox is a save-for-later app and bookmark manager that fixes this. One shared inbox for links, notes, images, screenshots, PDFs and files. Collections and tags to keep it tidy. Blazing-fast offline search — with OCR text recognition — to find anything in seconds. And reminders, so the things you saved actually come back to you.

Unlike cloud bookmark services, PettiBox is offline-first and private: no account to create, no subscription to pay, and your data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up to your own Google Drive.

Google Drive usage

Google Drive access is optional and used only for PettiBox backup and restore. PettiBox does not access unrelated Google Drive files. See the Privacy Policy for details.

FAQ

Is PettiBox free?
Yes — free on Google Play, with no ads and no subscription. PettiBox is a non-profit side project by an independent developer. Core features work fully offline and require no account.
What can I save in PettiBox?
Links, notes, images, screenshots, PDFs and files — shared from Chrome, YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, WhatsApp, TikTok, X, Gallery, Files and any Android app with a share button.
Can PettiBox search inside images and PDFs?
Yes. On-device OCR text recognition lets you search words that appear inside saved screenshots, images and PDFs.
Does PettiBox access my Google Drive files?
Google Drive access is optional and used only for PettiBox's own backup and restore. It never accesses unrelated Drive files.
