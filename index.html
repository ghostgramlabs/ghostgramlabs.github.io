A note from the developer

GhostGram Labs isn't a company. It's just the name I publish my side projects under. There's no office, no team, no investors — and no business model, on purpose. These apps are built in my own free time, with my own hands and the help of modern AI tools, and shared non-profit so anyone can use them for free. Each one started because something in my daily life was annoying enough to fix: links kept getting lost in chat threads, so PettiBox happened. Moving one video to an iPhone shouldn't need a cloud account, so DirectServe happened. A silent notification never reminded anyone to take their medicine, so SpeakAlert happened.

Because I use these apps every day myself, they stay the way I like my own tools: fast, offline, and quiet. No ads, no accounts, no "premium" nag screens. If something's broken or missing, email me at ghostgramlabs@gmail.com — the person who wrote the code is the one who reads it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about GhostGram Labs and our Android apps.

Are GhostGram Labs apps free?
Yes, completely. GhostGram Labs is not a company — these are non-profit side projects by an independent developer. PettiBox, DirectServe and SpeakAlert are free on Google Play with no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases and no hidden charges, because the goal is simply that people get useful tools for free.
Do your apps need an account or internet connection?
No account is ever required. Our apps are offline-first: everything stays on your device. Internet or local WiFi is only used for optional features like link previews (PettiBox), Google Drive backup (PettiBox) or local network transfer (DirectServe).
Which app should I use to move files from Android to iPhone?
DirectServe. It transfers files over WiFi or hotspot to any device with a browser — iPhone, iPad, PC, Mac, Chromebook or smart TV — with no app installation needed on the receiving side. No AirDrop, no cables, no cloud.
Can SpeakAlert speak reminders when my phone is locked?
Yes. SpeakAlert plays your recorded voice or text-to-speech reminders with alarm-style playback at the scheduled time, even when your device is locked or the app is in the background.
What can I save in PettiBox?
Links, notes, images, screenshots, PDFs and files — shared straight from Chrome, YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, WhatsApp, TikTok, X, Gallery, Files and any Android app that supports sharing. Organize with collections and tags, then find everything with fast offline search and OCR.
How do I get support or request a feature?
Email us at ghostgramlabs@gmail.com — we read everything.
