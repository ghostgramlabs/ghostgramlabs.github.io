Why SpeakAlert?

Standard reminder apps drop a silent notification in your tray — and by evening it's buried under twenty others. SpeakAlert takes a different approach: it says your reminder out loud, like a talking alarm. When your phone announces "take your medicine" in your own voice, you don't scroll past it.

SpeakAlert combines three kinds of alerts: voice recordings (hear exactly what you recorded), text-to-speech (type it, your phone speaks it) and custom audio (use any music or sound file). Schedule them once or on daily, weekly, monthly and custom repeats — ideal for medicines, habits, appointments, and reminders for family members who find phones hard to read.

And like every GhostGram Labs app, it's a personal side project that respects you: completely free, no ads, no account, fully offline. Your recordings and reminders never leave your device.

FAQ

Does SpeakAlert work when my phone is locked?
Yes — reminders play with alarm-style playback at the scheduled time, even when the device is locked or the app is in the background.
Can I use my own voice?
Yes. Record a voice note and SpeakAlert plays exactly what you recorded. You can also use text-to-speech or any audio file on your phone.
Is it good for medicine reminders?
Very. Daily, weekly, monthly and custom repeats fit medication schedules, spoken alerts are hard to miss, and the missed-reminder log lets you double-check anytime.
Is SpeakAlert really free?
Completely free — no ads, no subscriptions, no hidden charges. SpeakAlert is a hobby project by an independent developer. It works fully offline with no account, and your data stays on your device.
